    Our Mission

    We started with a very simple mission: to make sure that no one on
    campus would go hungry and no student would be stuck eating alone.
    By anonymously matching those with excess meal plan to those who
    ran out early, we wanted to foster friendships and mentorships.
    People loved it! And to our surprise, people found more value
    in thier newfound friendships than the meals they shared.
    That's when we knew we had something special. Our focus now is
    on fostering such friendships in local communities with interest-based
    anonymous matching and chatting. We want real friendships...
    knocking down the walls that are created by platforms focused on pictures
    and likes.
